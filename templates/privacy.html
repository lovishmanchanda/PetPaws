Pet Paws - Privacy Policy

At Pet Paws, we respect your privacy and are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as your name, email, phone number, home address, and pet adoption preferences. Additionally, we store information related to your adoption history and interactions with our platform.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your information to facilitate pet adoptions, improve our services, and provide you with important updates regarding pet care, upcoming events, and new adoption opportunities.

3. Protection of Your Data

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, modification, or misuse. Your information is securely stored and accessed only when necessary.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. However, we may share necessary details with veterinarians, adoption agencies, or local shelters to complete the adoption process responsibly.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us track site usage, optimize content, and personalize your interactions. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. If you wish to make changes or request data deletion, please contact us.

7. Compliance with Laws

We adhere to all applicable pet adoption and data protection laws. By using our platform, you agree to our data policies.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in regulations or improvements in our services. Please review this page periodically.

If you have any concerns about privacy and data protection, feel free to reach out to us.
