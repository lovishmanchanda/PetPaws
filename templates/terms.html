Pet Paws - Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Pet Paws! By using our pet adoption platform, you agree to the following terms and conditions.

1. Adoption Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old to adopt a pet. Proof of identification may be required.

2. Adoption Process

All adoptions go through a screening process to ensure pets are placed in responsible and caring homes. We reserve the right to refuse adoption if we determine the pet's well-being is at risk.

3. Responsibilities

Adopters agree to provide lifelong care, proper nutrition, shelter, medical attention, and companionship for their adopted pets.

4. Liability

Pet Paws is not responsible for any damages, injuries, or issues arising from pet adoption. Pet ownership is a personal responsibility.

5. Updates to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Please review this page periodically for updates.

6. Adoption Fees

Adoption fees are non-refundable and contribute to the care, medical needs, and well-being of our rescued animals.

7. Compliance with Local Laws

Adopters must comply with all local and national laws regarding pet ownership, including licensing and vaccination requirements.

8. Return Policy

If you can no longer care for your pet, please contact Pet Paws to discuss the best course of action. Abandoning adopted pets is strictly prohibited.

9. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and will not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

If you have any questions, please contact us.
